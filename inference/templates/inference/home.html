Carnivore Conservation Genomics

A conservation research project by PUCRS's genomics lab

Help us protect our wildlife using genetic data

This platform aims to connect conservation efforts for carnivore species in South and Central America.

  • Create a common database of genetic data, both full sequence and SNPs
  • Facilitate the creation of standardized SNP panels for geographic assignment
  • Make available SNP based tools for geographic assignment of samples

Your contributions support ongoing research and conservation efforts. Please select a species below to get started.
